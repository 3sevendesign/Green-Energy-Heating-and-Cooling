About Us

Green Energy Plumbing & Cooling is based in Kingston, NY and has been serving homeowners throughout the Hudson Valley for years. The business focuses on the installation and repair of plumbing, heating, and cooling systems — done with care, honesty, and the skill that comes with holding the City of Kingston Licensed Master Plumber designation.
